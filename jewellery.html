Project Description

Jewellery Calculation App for Jewellery (Gold) Business based on Android. Jewellery Calculator is your all-in-one tool for smart jewellery decisions!

The main features of this project is:
  • Calculate jewellery price INSTANTLY based on weight.
  • Calculate MOJURI Cost of Jewellery Making
  • Convert GRAMS to VORI and VORI to GRAMs effortlessly.
  • Plan your jewellery budget like a pro!.
  • Summation & Substraction Easily.
  • Become a confident jewellery buyer & seller.
  • Track your calculations with a detailed log report.
